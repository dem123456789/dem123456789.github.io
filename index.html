I am an international student from China. I was born in southwest China, a city called Chengdu which are well known for panda and delicious food. I am interested in signal processing and data analytics. I believe signal is the representation of real world and with data analytics we can understand the world better. Audio, imagery and other human senses connect us with the environment. We absorb information through these senses, process it with our own brain, and finally react upon the results. I am working on a mobile application in which people without any music knowledge can compose music with their own voice. Human voice is the simplest musical instrument that we can play any time anywhere.
